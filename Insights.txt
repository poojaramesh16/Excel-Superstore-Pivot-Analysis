Business Insights from Superstore Analysis

1. Technology category contributes the highest total sales among all categoreis , making it main revenue driver.
2. The west region shows consistently strong performance across all customer segments.
3. corporate segment generates higher sales compared to Home Office in most regions .
4. Furniture Category has lower profit margins compared to Technology and Office Supplies.
5. Central Region shows relatively weaker performance in both sales and profit, indicating scope for improvement.
